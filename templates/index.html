About the Event

AWS Student Community Day Jalandhar 2025 is the premier annual conference for student developers passionate about cloud computing, AI, and innovation. Hosted by the AWS Cloud Club at Lovely Professional University, this event brings together over 500 students, educators, and industry leaders for a day of learning, networking, and hands-on experiences.

Attendees will explore the latest in AWS technologies, including serverless computing, machine learning, cybersecurity, and Web3. The conference features keynote speeches from industry experts, technical sessions led by AWS professionals and community members, interactive workshops, and panel discussions on emerging trends in technology.

Whether you're a beginner looking to get started with cloud or an experienced developer aiming to deepen your skills, AWS Student Community Day Jalandhar 2025 offers something for everyone. Join us for a day of inspiration, education, and community building as we explore the future of technology together.

Agenda Highlights

Opening Keynote: India’s Tech Revolution

Exploring how AI, Cloud, and emerging technologies are transforming India’s digital future and shaping tomorrow’s innovators.

Session Track: Building with AWS

Dive into AI, Web3, Cybersecurity, and Cloud with live demos, real-world case studies, and speaker-led hands-on sessions.

Workshop: From Code to Cloud

Hands-on with AWS - build, deploy, and scale serverless applications using Lambda, API Gateway, and more.

Panel: The Future of Tech in India

A discussion on AI, Cloud, Web3, and Cybersecurity - industry leaders share insights on innovation, careers, and challenges in India.

Student Activities & Challenges

Engage in quizzes, challenges, and AWS-powered activities. Top performers win AWS Credits and exclusive rewards.

Networking & Closing Ceremony

Meet speakers, mentors, and fellow builders. Celebrate achievements and wrap up the day with community highlights.
