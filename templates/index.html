About the Event

AWS Student Community Day Jalandhar 2025 is the premier annual conference for student developers passionate about cloud computing, AI, and innovation.

Hosted by the AWS Cloud Club at Lovely Professional University, this event connects over 500 builders, educators, and industry leaders for a full day of learning, networking, and inspiration.

Dive deep into the world of AI, Web3, Serverless, and Cybersecurity through workshops, keynotes, and panel discussions that prepare you for the future of tech.
